About Us

Bharathiyar Tours and Travels

Travel is an experience to be felt through the senses, and it completes a person. What began as one man’s passion for driving and discovering breathtaking destinations has now grown into a trusted travel service delivering safe, comfortable, and memorable journeys.

I felt elated when driving to a destination to enjoy a breathtaking view. That feeling inspired me to build Bharathiyar Tours and Travels in 2018 at Coimbatore.
Our Journey

How it all started

From a single car to a growing team built on passion, service, and trust.

Our Beginning

Travel is an experience to be felt through the senses, and it completes a person. The joy of driving to a destination and witnessing a breathtaking view inspired the foundation of Bharathiyar Tours and Travels in 2018 at Coimbatore. Since then, we have been giving people the bliss of reaching their dream destinations while pursuing our passion for travel and driving.

How “I” Became “We”

At the age of 26, our founder bought his own car — the very first investment in this industry. As the days progressed, consistent performance and quality service helped expand the business. What started as an individual effort gradually transformed into a dedicated team that shares the same commitment to excellence and customer care.

Our Motto

Our motto is to take care of our clients’ safety and comfort from the moment they step into our arena. Our team of chauffeurs strives hard to provide an experience they will never forget. Our bond with clients continues even after their trip has ended, and that is why many of them return to us again for future travel.

What We Do

Our team takes care of the complete planning of the trip to keep every client safe, comfortable, and worry-free — not only while travelling, but also when choosing a destination, booking a stay, and organizing the journey. You name the destination, and we will take care of the rest.

Our Service

What you can expect from us

We focus on comfort, care, quality, and travel experiences that stay with you.

  • Safe and hassle-free trips.
  • Sanitized and tidy vehicles.
  • Professional and polite chauffeurs.
  • Specially curated itineraries.
  • Magnificent destinations.
  • Memorable trips.
Looking Ahead

What the future holds for us

We are extremely grateful to our team and family who made it possible to reach our current status, even though there were rocky roads and uneven terrains in our journey. We wish to expand our business further, add more people to our team, and at the same time provide our guests with everlasting memories through every trip we plan and every destination we serve.
