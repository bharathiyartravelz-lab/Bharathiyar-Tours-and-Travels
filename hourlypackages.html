Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our local hourly packages and booking process.

The local hourly package covers 100 Kms or 12 Hours, whichever comes first. It is designed for local city usage and nearby travel needs.
Extra kilometre and extra hour charges may apply beyond the package limit. Final extra charges will be informed clearly based on the actual usage.
Toll, parking, permit and any special additional usage charges are generally extra unless specifically mentioned at the time of confirmation.
Click the Book Now button for available vehicles, fill in your details in the popup form, and your enquiry will be sent directly through WhatsApp.
